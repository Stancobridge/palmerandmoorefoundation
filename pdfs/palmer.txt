ABOUT PALMER AND MOORE POVERTY
ALLEVIATION FOUNDATION
Rc: 7257545
Founded in 2023 with a strong dedication to elevate the underprivileged and
disenfranchised segments of society. Palmer and Moore is a renowned non-governmental
organization with its headquarters located in Abuja, Nigeria. Our purpose is driven by the
values of action, empathy and compassion, which direct us to positively influence both
spiritual and physical emancipation.
Main Aims:
Physical liberation: we are committed to helping those struggling financially by
launching programs that provide them access to basic needs, knowledge and training. Our
initiatives set the stage for long-term development and empowerment.
Spiritual Liberation:
Understanding the importance of spiritual health, we provide faith based programs,
therapy, and mentorship to assist people in overcoming emotional and spiritual obstacles,
promoting inner healing and rekindling hope.
Palmer and Moore make a significant effort to build robust and resilient communities.
We enable people to help one another by holding seminars, support groups and
community-building events providing cohesion and direction.

solutions, education and economic opportunities.

Our Focus Areas
1. Education, Providing access to quality education, vocational training and service
programs.
2. Economic Empowerment supporting entrepreneurship, job creation and financial
inclusive initiatives.
3. Health and Wellness:
Improving access to healthcare, sanitation, and hygiene facilities.
4. Communities Development:
Community institutions, promoting social cohesion and supporting infrastructure
development.
Our Approach
1. Community-led: we work closely with local communities chiefs to identify their
needs and develop tailored solutions.
2. Partnerships: we collaborate with governments, NGO, businesses, and individuals
to leverage resources and expertise.
3. Sustainability: we prioritize sustainable solutions that promote long-term
economic growth and social impact.
4. Mentorship and evaluation: we regularly assess our programs to ensure
effectiveness and impact.
