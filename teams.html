Meet Our Dedicated Team

Combining expertise, passion, and commitment to eradicate poverty and empower local communities.

Chairman / CEO

Michael Ibeabuchi Nkama

With over 25 years of experience in international development and social economics, Michael Ibeabuchi established the foundation with a singular vision: to create sustainable pathways out of poverty.

Her pioneering work in community-led development has been recognized globally, focusing on the intersection of education, healthcare access, and economic empowerment. "Change doesn't happen for people, it happens with people," she often reminds the team.

"Our mission is to plant seeds of hope that grow into forests of self-sufficiency."
