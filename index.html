Our Mission

To create sustainable pathways out of poverty by providing essential resources, vocational training, and financial support to underserved Nigerian communities.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where every citizen, regardless of their background, has the opportunity to thrive and contribute to a prosperous nation.

A Message From Our Founder

"Poverty is not just a lack of money; it is a lack of opportunity and hope. At Palmer & Moore, we believe that by investing in people—through education, healthcare, and small business support—we can break the cycles of generational poverty that have held back our communities for too long."

"Our commitment to Nigeria is unwavering. Together, with our donors and volunteers, we are building a foundation of strength, one community at a time."

Our Recent Impact

Transforming lives across the federation through strategic intervention and grassroots engagement.

Education

Floating School Grant

Providing digital tools and school supplies to over 500 students in underserved waterfront communities.

Healthcare

Rural Health Outreach

Mobile clinics reaching 12 remote villages, providing essential vaccinations and maternal care.

Empowerment

Women's Micro-Credit Fund

Empowering 200 widows with interest-free loans to start sustainable tailoring and catering businesses.

Vocational Training

Tech Skills Vocational Hub

A training center providing coding and hardware repair skills for unemployed youth.

Clean Water

Borehole Water Project

Installing solar-powered water systems in 5 communities for safe drinking water.

Agriculture

Smallholder Farmer Grants

Distributing fertilizers, high-yield seeds, and tractor access to 300 farmers across rural communities.
