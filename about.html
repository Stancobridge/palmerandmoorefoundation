Empowering Communities, Transforming Lives

Founded in 2023, Palmer & Moore Poverty Alleviation Foundation is dedicated to elevating the underprivileged and disenfranchised segments of society through sustainable solutions.

Our Mission

To empower marginalized communities to break the cycle of poverty through sustainable solutions, education, and economic opportunities.

Our Vision

A vision where everyone has access to basic necessities, economic opportunities, and social services free from the shackles of poverty.

Who We Are

Palmer and Moore Poverty Alleviation Foundation (Rc: 7257545) is a renowned non-governmental organization based in Nigeria. Established in 2023, our purpose is driven by the values of Action, Empathy, and Compassion, which direct us to positively influence both spiritual and physical emancipation.

We are committed to helping those struggling financially by launching programs that provide access to basic needs, knowledge, and training. Our initiatives set the stage for long-term development and empowerment.

Understanding the importance of spiritual health, we also provide faith-based programs, therapy, and mentorship to assist people in overcoming emotional and spiritual obstacles, promoting inner healing and rekindling hope.
